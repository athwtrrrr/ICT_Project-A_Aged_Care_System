Our Services

At ACMS, we provide comprehensive care solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of each resident. Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals and support staff work together to ensure a safe, comfortable, and dignified living environment that promotes independence, well-being, and quality of life for all our residents.

Need to Book Accommodation for a Loved One?

We make it easy to reserve a room or schedule a tour of our facilities. Our friendly staff are ready to assist you with the transition process.

24/7 Nursing Care

Round-the-clock access to trained medical professionals who provide continuous monitoring and support for all healthcare needs.

Medication Management

Accurate administration and monitoring of prescribed medications, ensuring proper dosages and timing for optimal health outcomes.

Personal Care Assistance

Compassionate help with daily hygiene, grooming, bathing, and dressing needs, maintaining dignity and independence.

Meal Services

Nutritious and delicious meals prepared by professional chefs with dietary needs in mind, served in a restaurant-style dining room.

Therapy & Rehabilitation

Physical, occupational, and speech therapy programs designed to maintain mobility, functionality, and quality of life.

Activity Programs

Engaging social, recreational, and creative activities that stimulate the mind, foster connections, and promote emotional well-being.

Mobility Assistance

Professional support for residents with mobility challenges, including walking aids, transfer assistance, and customized mobility plans.

Emergency Response

24/7 rapid response system for emergencies with immediate access to medical assistance and transportation when needed.

Residential Care Services

Our residential care facilities provide comprehensive support in a comfortable, home-like environment designed for seniors who require assistance with daily activities and healthcare needs.

  • Permanent Accommodation: Long-term living arrangements with personalized care plans
  • Respite Care: Short-term stays providing temporary relief for primary caregivers
  • Memory Care: Specialized programs for residents with dementia or Alzheimer's disease
  • Palliative Care: Compassionate end-of-life care focused on comfort and dignity
  • Secure Environment: Safe living spaces with appropriate supervision and security measures

In-Home Care Services

Our in-home care services allow seniors to maintain independence while receiving necessary support in the comfort of their own homes.

  • Personal Care: Assistance with bathing, dressing, grooming, and toileting
  • Household Support: Help with light housekeeping, laundry, and meal preparation
  • Medication Supervision: Reminders and assistance with medication management
  • Transportation: Accompaniment to medical appointments and social activities
  • Companionship: Regular visits providing social interaction and emotional support

Health & Wellness Services

We provide comprehensive health and wellness services designed to maintain and improve the physical and mental well-being of our residents.

  • Regular Health Assessments: Ongoing monitoring of vital signs and health conditions
  • Specialized Dietary Plans: Customized nutrition programs addressing individual health needs
  • Fitness Programs: Gentle exercise classes tailored to different mobility levels
  • Mental Health Support: Counseling and social activities to promote emotional well-being
  • Coordination with Specialists: Management of external healthcare appointments and services

Frequently Asked Questions

What levels of care do you provide?
We offer various levels of care, including independent living with minimal assistance, assisted living with daily support, and comprehensive care for those with complex health needs. Each resident receives a personalized care plan based on their specific requirements.
How do you ensure the safety of residents?
Our facilities are equipped with 24/7 monitoring systems, emergency call buttons in all rooms, secure entry/exit points, and round-the-clock staffing. We conduct regular safety drills and maintain strict infection control protocols.
What is the process for booking accommodation?
The process begins with an initial inquiry, followed by a needs assessment and facility tour. Once we understand the specific needs, we work with families to find the most suitable accommodation and develop a personalized care plan. You can start the process by using our online booking form.
Do you accept insurance or government subsidies?
Yes, we work with various insurance providers and accept eligible government subsidies. Our administrative team can help you understand what financial assistance options are available and guide you through the application process.
How can family members stay involved in care decisions?
We believe in collaborative care and encourage family involvement. We hold regular care plan meetings, provide secure online portals for families to access updates, and welcome family visits. Our staff is always available to discuss any concerns or questions.
