Frequently Asked Questions

ACMS provides a full spectrum of care ranging from independent living with minimal assistance to comprehensive nursing care for those with complex medical needs. Each resident receives a personalized care plan tailored to their specific requirements, which is regularly reviewed and adjusted as needs change.

Our licensed nursing staff manages all medication needs according to physician orders. We maintain accurate records of all medications administered, ensure proper storage, and coordinate with pharmacies for timely refills. Our team also monitors for potential side effects and efficacy, communicating regularly with healthcare providers about any concerns.

We offer a diverse range of activities designed to engage residents physically, mentally, and socially. These include exercise classes, art therapy, music programs, games, outings to local attractions, educational sessions, and seasonal celebrations. Our activities team creates monthly calendars with options suitable for various interests and abilities.

Our dining services team, led by professional chefs and dietitians, creates nutritious meals that accommodate various dietary restrictions including diabetic, low-sodium, gluten-free, and texture-modified diets. We regularly update our menu options and can work with residents and their healthcare providers to meet specific nutritional requirements while maintaining quality and flavor.

We offer comprehensive therapy services including physical therapy to improve mobility and strength, occupational therapy to enhance daily living skills, and speech therapy for communication and swallowing difficulties. Our therapy team works closely with each resident to develop personalized rehabilitation plans aimed at maximizing independence and quality of life.
